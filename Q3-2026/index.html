ACTION 1: BUILD A SUBSCRIBER AUDIENCE VIA AMAZON MARKETING CLOUD (AMC) Use AMC to create a custom rule-based audience of existing Collagen subscribers (Campaign Manager → Measurement & Reporting → Amazon Marketing Cloud → Audiences). This audience can then be applied as a bid modifier within PPC campaigns — increasing bids specifically for shoppers who are already subscribed, to surface complementary products more aggressively to a warm, high-intent group. A prior attempt at a broader "frequent purchasers not on Subscribe & Save" audience did not meet Amazon's minimum audience size threshold. The subscriber-specific audience is more targeted and likely to qualify given subscriber count has grown from 3,773 to 6,281 (+66%) — should be revisited and tested with current volume. If a query doesn't pass Amazon's review, it can be revised and resubmitted. ACTION 2: SUBSCRIBER RETARGETING CAMPAIGNS Layer the AMC subscriber audience into Sponsored Display campaigns with an increased bid modifier. Creative and copy should reinforce the Bone Renewal Ritual, positioning Annatrol as "the next step," not a separate purchase. Use Sponsored Brands video/custom image placements to tell the "Foundation → Advanced Support" story in a single ad unit. ACTION 3: PRIORITISE ANNATROL AS THE EXPANSION PRODUCT Increase Annatrol PPC budget allocation given its 60% YoY growth and emerging subscription base. Test "Complete the Ritual" bundle messaging in Sponsored Brands headlines. ACTION 4: CROSS-SELL VIA BRANDED DEFENSE + EXPANSION COMBO Maintain strong branded campaign coverage on Collagen (protects subscriber funnel from competitor takeover). Layer in product-targeting campaigns where Annatrol, Creatine + HMB, and Electrolytes ads appear on Collagen's product page placements (PAT campaigns).
